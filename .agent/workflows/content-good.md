Thorough copy creation with research and multiple iterations.

## Workflow

1. **Brief analysis** — understand audience, goals, tone, constraints
2. **Research** — study competitors, industry language, best examples
3. **Draft** — write 2-3 variants
4. **Refine** — polish best variant
5. **Deliver** — final copy with rationale and usage notes

## Notes
- Research-backed copy creation
- Multiple variants for comparison
- Includes rationale for creative choices
