Review existing content, identify issues, and improve it.

## Workflow

1. **Read** the current content
2. **Analyze** for: clarity, engagement, tone, structure, SEO, readability
3. **Identify** specific issues and weaknesses
4. **Enhance** the content preserving the original intent
5. **Report** changes made and rationale

## Notes
- Preserve original voice and intent
- Focus on clarity and engagement
- Sacrifice grammar for concision in reports
