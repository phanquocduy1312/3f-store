Thorough design process with research and refinement.

## Workflow

1. **Analyze** requirements (purpose, audience, tone, constraints)
2. **Research** design trends, inspiration, references
3. **Activate** `frontend-design` skill for aesthetic direction
4. **Activate** `ui-ux-pro-max` skill for style/typography/color selection
5. **Implement** with attention to typography, color, motion, spatial composition
6. **Generate** visual assets with `ai-multimodal` skill if needed
7. **Refine** details (hover states, transitions, responsive behavior)
8. **Verify** quality and deliver

## Notes
- Research-driven design decisions
- Distinctive, non-generic aesthetics
- Full attention to micro-interactions and details
