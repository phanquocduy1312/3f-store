Archive completed or stale plans and write journal entries.

## Workflow

1. **Scan** `./plans/` for completed or stale plans
2. **Write** journal entries for completed work in `./docs/journals/`
3. **Archive** specified plans (move to `./plans/archive/` or mark as archived)
4. **Report** what was archived

## Notes
- Journal entries capture key decisions and outcomes
- Archiving keeps `./plans/` directory clean
- Can archive specific plans or all completed ones
